About

Who we are

Welcome to THE HOME LIVINGS,We believe in high quality products. Your #1 source for all handmade Furnitures. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of home decor products with durability and home friendly, Also we make all types of hand made furniture items of wood, iron and bone as per your requirement.

Founded in 2012, THE HOME LIVINGS has come a long way from its beginnings in a Jodhpur. When store founder first started out, his passion for providing Indian handmade crafts to each country. And gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the handicraft industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
